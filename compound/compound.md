# Projects to learn compound interest

Background: compound interest is a way to accrue money simply by putting it in a place where it gets interest.

## Step 1: money doubling

1. Start with $0.01 (1 cent)
2. Every day, double it
3. Find out how many dollars you have after 30 days (1 month)

## Step 2: How long does it take to make a million dollars?

1. Start with $0.01 (1 cent)
2. Double your money every day
3. Stop when you have $1 million dollars

## Step 3: Variable interests

1. Start with $0.01 (1 cent)
2. Instead of doubling every day, try a different rate (doubling everyday is called '100% daily')
3. Realistically, interest rate in the US is about 4% per year
